A memorial concert for the Namibian musician Tauna Niingungo, performed at the Museum of Ethnography in Stockholm.

Below is the exact running order of the choir songs and solos.

## Choir songs (approx. 15 min total)

1. Thuma mina -- ~3:30
2. Vesa vesa -- ~2:00
3. Kothbiro
4. Umama Maliki -- ~2:30
5. Marit's song to Tauna -- ~2:30
6. Oshi Kuku Vanda (Tauna Niingungo) -- ~3:00

## Solo songs (approx. 25 min total)

1. En stund på jorden -- ~4:30
2. Do you know -- Osibisa -- ~5:00
3. Needed time -- Eric Bibb -- ~4:00

5. Mamadou Sene -- _Fela_ -- ~6:00

2. Woulaba -- Habib Koité -- ~4:30
3. Satta Massagana -- The Abyssinians -- ~6:00

2. 🎶 We shall not be moved (Trad., sing-along) -- ~3:00
3. 🎶 Nkosi Sikeleli Afrika (God Bless Africa) -- ~2:30
4. 👧🏾👦🏾 Tauna's children's words to us -- ~3:00
