After a round of sketching, we've landed on a visual direction we call **Jordsång** ("earth song"). It lives at the meeting point between Swedish folk simplicity and African warmth — terracotta, ochre, moss green on cream. Hand-stamped, warm, collective.

## The colors

- **Primary — Terracotta** for the logo and key accents
- **Secondary — Forest** for supporting elements
- **Accent — Ochre** for small highlights

## Typography

Instrument Serif for headings — a humanist serif with hand-drawn warmth. DM Sans for body text — clean, friendly, neutral.

More coming soon — posters, social cards, a whole world built around Jordsång.
